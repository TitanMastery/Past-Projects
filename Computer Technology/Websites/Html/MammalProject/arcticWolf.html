Arctic Wolves

The Arctic wolf is a subspecies of graf wolf that lives on the Canadian Arctic Archipelago between Melville Island and Ellesmere Island. Another name for the Arctic wolf is the Melville Island wolf.

Arctic Wolf Diet:

  • Muskoxen
  • Arctic Hares
  • Lemmings
  • Arctic Foxes
  • Birds
  • Beetles
