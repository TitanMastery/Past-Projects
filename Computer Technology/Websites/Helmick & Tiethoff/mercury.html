Mercury


Top 10 Facts!

  1. 1st planet from the sun
  2. A day on mercury is equivalent to 176 Earth days
  3. Mercury has one the fastest orbits around the sun with each year taking only 88 Earth days
  4. Mercury is the smallest planet in the solar system. It’s diameter is only 4,879 km
  5. Mercury’s density is 5.4 grams which makes it the second most dense planet in our solar system
  6. Mercury is the second hottest planet in the solar system with temperatures reaching up to 427 degrees fahrenheit
  7. Mercury has more craters than any other planet in the solar system due to its thin atmosphere
  8. Mercury’s pull of gravity is only 38% of that on Earth
  9. Mercury’s atmosphere is very thin because it is so close to the sun. The solar winds destroy the small atmosphere it has but also replenish some of the gases it lost
  10. Mercury’s surface appears to be wrinkled due to the cooling of its core. As the core cooled and shrunk the surface contracted in on itself create these “wrinkles”

Three Bonus Facts!

  1. Rings: Mercury dosn't have any rings
  2. Distance From The Sun: 35.98 million mi
  3. Namesake: Mercury, Roman Messenger of the Gods

Satellite Info

Mercury doesn’t have any natural satellites but researchers have launched one successful artificial satellite to it, called MESSENGER
