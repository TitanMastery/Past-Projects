Bridal Party Services

Party Services

On your wedding day, you'll have plenty to worry about other than your hair and make-up. Let the technicians at Head to Toe Day Spa and Salon help put your mind at ease. As one of the most established day spas and salons in Wilmington, NC, we welcome local and out-of-town bridal parties for pre-wedding treatments and styling. Let us help your day to be memorable.

Wedding Day Services

At Head to Toe Day Spa and Salon, we specialize in helping every bride get ready for her special day in style. From head to toe, we have you covered. Our large salon, nail room, massage and skin-care rooms can accommodate large parties preparing for the big event. We can handle all of your pre-wedding treatments, including:

  • Manicures and pedicures
  • Facials
  • Waxing and tinting
  • Sunless airbrush tanning
  • Make-up application
  • Hair styling for the bride, wedding party and any pre-wedding activities
  • Massages

Relax in Comfort

Bring your bridal party for a day of relaxation and pampering. Whether it's just the bride or the whole wedding party, we invite you to enjoy your experience. Feel free to bring hors d'oeuvres and drinks.

Payment Terms

We will retain your credit card number upon booking your reservation.

The balance may be paid on the day of the event.

A 20% gratuity will be included for parties of four or more.

We are happy to offer any suggestions for treatments prior to your special event. If you are having your hair and make-up done for your wedding, we highly suggest having a trial-run prior to the day of to ease additional stress on the big day.
