Welcome

Welcome to Head to Toe Day Spa & Salon in Wilmington, NC where our passion is providing exceptional service for all of your beauty needs. Our salon and spa offers a friendly and inviting atmosphere and our staff is dedicated to providing you with a truly rewarding experience.

Whether you're looking for a new look or you want to be pampered, our talented team will take the time to listen to your needs to ensure that you leave feeling and looking great. Our staff has been trained in the latest and techniques and is well versed in the best professional products in the industry to give you stunning results every time.

Every guest at our salon and spa is special to us and we appreciate being given the opportunity to service your beauty needs. Make an appointment soon. We look forward to serving you.
