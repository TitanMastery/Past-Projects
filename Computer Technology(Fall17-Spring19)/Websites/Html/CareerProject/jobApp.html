Job Application

The Job:

Columbia Insurance Group has an open position for a web developer. They are looking for someone who can code very complicated applications and websites. Also, they want the developer to be able to work with others and create and manage application projects. They require the developer to have a bachelor's degree, good communication skills, previous experience with Java and Java EE, and knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. They would also like the developer to have previous experience with JSF, SQL, jQuery, and Websphere, but it is not required.


How To Get It:

To prepare for this job, you could get an online Bachelor of Science in Information Technology degree with an Advanced Software Developer Certificate from The University of Phoenix. This degree will give you all of the training you need for the position except the three years of Java experience. To get the experience, you could work for the state as an information technology specialist after the first year of your degree. You would continue to work there for the next three years and then apply at Columbia Insurance Group.
