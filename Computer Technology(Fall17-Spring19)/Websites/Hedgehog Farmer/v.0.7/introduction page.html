Welcome to Hedgehog Farmer inc

You're the new farmer in town and it's you're job to raise these little hedgehogs and some how make a living off it. it won't be easy they eat food so you'll have to feed them and your old farm will need more bowls for them and you will have to buy extra land all of theses can be found in the shop if your ever in need of money you can sell a couple of your hedgehogs but make sure to always keep at least 2 or more in even numbers so you can breed them if you forget to feed them they will die and if there is not enough space they will fight over it when your ready to play press the play button you cant miss it.
